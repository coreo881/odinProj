My famous pizza recipe

Description

This pizza was passed down to me by the gods in Haven. Yes. Haven.

Ingredients

  • Wet dough
  • The king's arugula
  • Stone filtered tomato paste
  • Glass filtered tomato sauce
  • One large pepperoni the size of a dinner plate
  • cheese

Equipment

  • Small bowl
  • Medium bowl
  • Large bowl
  • Ladle
  • Hands
  • Pizza stone*
  • a Gaggenau 400 Series BS48 Combi-steam Oven
*Not a pizza made out of stone.

Steps

  1. Get your wet dough. I don't care how you got it wet.
  2. Roll that dough out until it's one inch in diameter larger than your large, dinner plate sized pepperoni.
  3. Put your tomato paste in your tomato sauce and mix it all up. Ladle mixture onto wet dough disc.
  4. Cheese it!
  5. Sneak into the king's kitchen after nightfall and whisper to Edelwynn, the master cook, that the "Greenery looks nice this time of night" to gain access to the storeroom.
  6. Pick up arugula and put on the saucy cheesy dough disc
  7. Place whole pepperoni onto that bad boy
  8. Place almost-pizza onto pizza stone* and into your Gaggenau 400 Series BS48 Combi-steam Oven
  9. Watch that baby go!
  10. Eat it.
  11. Keep eating it you monster.
*Not a pizza made out of stone.
